# Retention Diagnosis

<instructions>Diagnose retention problems</instructions>
<process>
1. Ask for current retention metrics (churn, NRR, DAU/MAU)
2. Analyze by cohort (signup date, plan, segment)
3. Identify churn patterns (timing, pre-churn behavior)
4. If the user has cohort/retention benchmarks (their own historicals, or industry data they've shared), compare against them. Otherwise ask, or offer to look up published benchmarks via `WebSearch` and cite each source inline. Do not invent retention numbers, NRR ranges, or churn thresholds.
5. Propose root-cause hypotheses
6. Recommend actions per segment
</process>
<output_format>Retention diagnosis with cohort analysis, causes, and action plan</output_format>
