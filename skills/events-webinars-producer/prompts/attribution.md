# Attribution & Reporting

<instructions>
Build the attribution and reporting plan for the event.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Distinguish three metrics: registered, attended, engaged. Define
   "engaged" precisely (e.g. attended ≥ 30 min, asked ≥ 1 question,
   or downloaded content).
2. Pipeline attribution: pre-post lift on registered list vs control
   (or shared-credit with paired AEs).
3. Cost per: cost-per-registration, cost-per-attended, cost-per-engaged.
4. Dashboard: where it lives, who reviews, day-of-week.
5. Recommend Pixel Metrics co-validate.
6. Write into `v{n}.md`.
</process>
