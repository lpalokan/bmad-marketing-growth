# Social Media Motion

<instructions>
Commission a multi-platform social motion. Delegates to Nova
(social-media-strategist) who coordinates the platform creators
(LinkedIn, YouTube) and Nova-owned platforms (Twitter, Reddit as
capabilities).
</instructions>

<process>
1. Confirm: motion length (sprint / quarter / always-on), platforms
   in scope, target ICP segment, content theme tied to a campaign or
   launch.
2. Issue brief using `brief-templates/social-motion.md`. Acceptance
   criteria include: platform mix with rationale, content calendar,
   measurement plan, brand-voice compliance.
3. Review per `docs/protocol.md`.
</process>
