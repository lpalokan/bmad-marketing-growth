# Customer Advisory Board Ops

<instructions>
Run the operations side of a Customer Advisory Board (CAB).
</instructions>

<process>
1. Composition: target N members, segment / industry mix, executive
   sponsor from our side per member.
2. Cadence: 2 in-person + 2 virtual per year (typical) — confirm
   with user.
3. Agenda framework: product roadmap input, strategic challenges,
   shared learning, peer networking.
4. Member care: rotation, recognition, calendar protection.
5. Output management: minutes, action items, owner.
6. Write into `v{n}.md`.
</process>
