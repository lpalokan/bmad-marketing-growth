# Case Study

<instructions>
Produce a case study from interview to publication-ready draft.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Pre-interview: customer profile, 3 key questions, success metric
   we want to surface. Send to CSM for review.
2. Interview script (30-45 min): situation, problem, why us, what
   changed, outcome (sourced — do not invent numbers; ask for
   sources).
3. Draft structure: customer one-pager (problem → why us → outcome
   → quote → CTA).
4. Approval flow: customer marketing → CSM → customer legal → ready
   to publish.
5. Distribution plan: where it lives (web), where it's used (sales
   bundle, PR, paid).
6. Write into `v{n}.md`.
</process>
