# Analyst Briefing Prep

<instructions>
Prepare a briefing deck and run-of-show for an analyst briefing.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Read brief, `positioning.md`, the analyst's recent published work,
   the prior briefing notes.
2. Map the agenda to the analyst's stated evaluation criteria
   (cite the doc URL).
3. Deck structure: market context (their view, our view) → strategy
   → product → customer evidence → roadmap → ask (what we want from
   the analyst).
4. Customer evidence: 2-3 named examples with permission, otherwise
   anonymous with segment.
5. Q&A prep: top 10 likely questions, especially the hard ones.
6. Write into `v{n}.md`.
</process>
