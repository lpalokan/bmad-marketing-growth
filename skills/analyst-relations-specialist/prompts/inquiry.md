# Customer-Inquiry Response

<instructions>
Prepare materials for a customer-inquiry (analyst inquiry on a deal).
</instructions>

<process>
1. Identify the inquiry context: deal stage, the analyst engaged,
   the buyer's evaluation criteria.
2. Materials: positioning one-pager, customer evidence, competitive
   teardown summary, roadmap excerpt.
3. Spokesperson assignment.
4. Post-inquiry: capture analyst's observations into the AR notes
   sidecar.
5. Write into `v{n}.md`.
</process>
