# Battle Card (1 page)

<instructions>
Produce a 1-page sales battle card derived from an existing teardown.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Read the source teardown from `work/`. If none exists for this
   competitor, refuse and ask the orchestrator to commission a CT
   first.
2. Structure (one page, table-heavy):
   - Header: competitor name, our positioning vs theirs, one-sentence
     summary.
   - "Why they win" (3 bullets, sourced).
   - "Why we win" (3 bullets, sourced).
   - Top 5 objections + suggested reframe (table).
   - Trap questions: 3 questions an AE can ask that surface the
     competitor's weak spots.
   - Pricing comparison (model only — list price moves too fast for
     a battle card).
   - DO NOT SAY: 3-5 disparaging phrases to avoid in deals.
3. Public-source claims only. Anything that needs internal evidence
   gets dropped or moved to an internal-only appendix.
4. Write `v{n}.md`.
</process>
