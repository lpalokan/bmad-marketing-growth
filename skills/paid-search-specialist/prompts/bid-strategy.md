# Bid Strategy & Pacing

<instructions>
Recommend the bid strategy and lay out budget pacing.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Per campaign: name the bid strategy (Manual CPC, Maximize Clicks,
   Target CPA, Target ROAS, Maximize Conversion Value). Justify
   against current conversion data — do not pick a smart-bidding
   strategy if the account lacks the conversion volume to train it
   (cite the conversion-volume threshold from Google's docs).
2. Budget pacing: weekly / daily caps per campaign; flagged when to
   raise caps based on quality-score and conversion thresholds.
3. Bidding rules: dayparting, geo bid modifiers (cite source for
   geo-level performance differences).
4. Write into `v{n}.md`.
</process>
