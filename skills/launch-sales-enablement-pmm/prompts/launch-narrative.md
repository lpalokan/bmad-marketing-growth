# Launch Narrative

<instructions>
Draft the launch narrative bundle per the brief.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Read brief, `positioning.md`, `icp.md`, `brand-voice.md`, any
   competitive teardowns linked from the brief.
2. Produce in this order (each is a discrete section in `v{n}.md`):
   a. One-line story (≤15 words). Frame as buyer change, not feature.
   b. 30-second elevator (≤80 words). No product name in the first
      sentence.
   c. Long-form narrative (300-500 words). Lead with the buyer.
   d. 3 supporting proof points with sources.
   e. Not-this list (anti-claims). At least 3 items.
3. Verify each section against `brand-voice.md` do/don't.
4. Verify each numeric claim has a source.
5. Write to `work/{brief_id}/v{n}.md`. Update `state.yaml`.
</process>
