# Brand Audit

<instructions>
Audit voice and narrative consistency across recent customer-facing
assets.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Ask user for: a sample of 10-20 recent assets (URLs, files, screen-
   shots) across channels.
2. Score each against `brand-voice.md` do/don't and the current brand
   narrative. Flag every miss with a specific quote and the rule
   broken.
3. Pattern: what's the systemic gap (e.g. "BDR email templates skew
   too informal", "PR press releases pile on jargon").
4. Recommend: which orchestrator owns the fix (Content / Digital /
   Field / PR).
5. Write to `work/{yyyy-mm-brand-audit}/v{n}.md`.
</process>
