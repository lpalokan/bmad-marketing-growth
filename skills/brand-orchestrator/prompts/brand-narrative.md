# Brand Narrative

<instructions>
Commission a brand narrative refresh. Delegates to
brand-narrative-strategist.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Confirm: trigger (new positioning, new fiscal year, repositioning,
   crisis), scope (full narrative vs. one chapter), target audience.
2. Issue brief using `brief-templates/brand-narrative.md`. Acceptance
   criteria include: through-line statement, narrative chapters
   mapped to funnel stages, sample asset per chapter, voice
   compliance, localization-readiness notes.
3. Review per `docs/protocol.md`.
</process>
