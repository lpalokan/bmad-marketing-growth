# Locale Rollout Plan

<instructions>
Build the per-locale rollout plan from the brief.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Read brief, `brand-voice.md`, `positioning.md`, `icp.md`.
2. Per locale (one section each):
   - Voice adaptation guide: what stays from the source-of-truth voice,
     what bends locally (with examples).
   - Cultural / market notes: what idioms / references will not work.
   - Asset inventory mapping (English source → locale target).
   - Translation vendor / freelancer assigned.
   - In-market reviewer named.
   - QA gate sign-off (linguistic + in-market + technical).
3. Hand-off to Tek Crawl for hreflang and indexation setup.
4. Write into `v{n}.md`.
</process>
