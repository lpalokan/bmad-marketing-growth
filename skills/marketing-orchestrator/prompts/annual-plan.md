# Annual Marketing Plan

<instructions>
Build the annual marketing plan: bets per domain, sequencing across
quarters, budget split, success criteria.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Read `kpis.md`, `positioning.md`, `icp.md`, the prior year's
   marketing-report (ask).
2. Confirm with the user: annual pipeline target, total marketing
   budget, headcount additions planned, board-level commitments.
3. Per quarter: name the 1-2 dominant motions (e.g. Q1: Product
   Launch X / Q2: Geo Expansion EMEA / Q3: Analyst Cycle / Q4:
   Demand Pull-forward).
4. Per domain: domain-level resourcing and 3-5 outcomes for the year.
5. Cross-domain dependencies: who blocks whom (e.g. PMM positioning
   must land before Digital paid campaign).
6. Pair with Pixel for the measurement framework.
7. Write to `work/{yyyy-annual-plan}/v{n}.md`. Surface to the user
   for sign-off and then issue per-domain quarterly briefs.
</process>
