# Paid Social / Demand-Gen Campaign

<instructions>
Commission a paid-social or demand-gen campaign across LinkedIn, Meta,
Google Demand Gen, YouTube Ads. Delegates to paid-social-demand-specialist.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Confirm: campaign type (demand creation vs lead capture), platform
   mix, budget split, target ICP segments, creative requirements,
   gated asset (if any), measurement plan.
2. Issue brief using `brief-templates/paid-demand.md`. Acceptance
   criteria: audience definition per platform, creative concept per
   audience, frequency cap, brand-safety controls, attribution model
   (lift study, MMM, or last-click), reporting cadence.
3. Review per `docs/protocol.md`.
</process>
