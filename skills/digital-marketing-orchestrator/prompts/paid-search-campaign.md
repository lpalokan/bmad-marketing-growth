# Paid Search Campaign

<instructions>
Commission a paid-search campaign (Google Ads, Bing Ads, PMax).
Delegates to paid-search-specialist.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Confirm: campaign objective (search, brand, competitor, PMax),
   budget, geo, target persona, landing page(s), tracking setup.
2. Issue brief using `brief-templates/paid-search.md`. Acceptance
   criteria: keyword tiering (brand / category / competitor / long-tail),
   negatives list, ad variants per ad group, landing page UTM plan,
   bid strategy, reporting cadence.
3. Review per `docs/protocol.md`.
</process>
