# Brand Voice

<instructions>
Define tone of voice and personal branding guidelines for social presence.
</instructions>
<process>
1. Understand founder/company personality and values
2. Define voice attributes (formal/casual, technical/accessible, etc.)
3. Create voice guidelines with do/don't examples
4. Adapt to platform conventions while maintaining consistency
5. Output: Brand voice document with platform-specific adaptations
</process>
