# Co-Marketing Campaign

<instructions>
Commission a co-marketing campaign with a partner. Delegates to
partner-marketplace-manager.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Confirm: partner, anchor moment (joint launch, webinar, event,
   content), joint pipeline target, partner-side contact.
2. Issue brief using `brief-templates/partner-program.md`. Acceptance
   criteria include: joint narrative balancing both positionings,
   channel mix, co-branding rules, attribution model.
3. Review per `docs/protocol.md`.
</process>
