# Quarterly Channel & Partner Plan

<instructions>
Build the quarterly channel plan: partners, programs, joint pipeline
targets.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Read `kpis.md`, current partner roster (ask), current marketplace
   listings (ask).
2. Per partner: status (tier, contract, exec sponsor), joint plan
   status, pipeline target this quarter.
3. Marketplace: listings to refresh, listings to launch (and the
   listing-fee approval status).
4. Channel enablement: gaps to close (training, decks, demos).
5. Write to `work/{yyyy-qN-channel-plan}/v{n}.md`. Surface to Max.
</process>
