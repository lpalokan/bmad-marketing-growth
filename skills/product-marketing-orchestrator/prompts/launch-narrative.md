# Launch Narrative

<instructions>
Build the launch narrative for a product, feature, or repositioning.
This is the upstream artefact the rest of the launch motion (PR, paid,
field, content) hangs off.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Qualify with the user: what's launching, why now, target audience,
   launch date, "must-not-miss" channels, and the one-sentence
   takeaway you want a buyer to repeat after seeing it.
2. Load `positioning.md`, `icp.md`, `brand-voice.md`.
3. Issue a brief to `launch-sales-enablement-pmm` using
   `brief-templates/launch-enablement.md`. Acceptance criteria MUST
   include:
   - one-line story (the "what changed for the buyer"),
   - 30-second elevator,
   - long-form narrative (300-500 words),
   - 3 supporting proof points,
   - explicit not-this list (anti-claims to avoid),
   - hand-off plan to PR Comms, Digital, Field for downstream execution.
4. Review per `docs/protocol.md`. On APPROVED, surface the narrative to
   Max (CMO) and propose follow-up workflows (`product-launch`
   workflow).
</process>
