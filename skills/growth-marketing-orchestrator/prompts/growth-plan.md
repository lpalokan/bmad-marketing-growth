# Quarterly Growth Plan

<instructions>
Build the quarterly growth plan: funnel targets per stage, the
experiment backlog, the lifecycle programs to launch or refresh.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Read `kpis.md`, `icp.md`, `positioning.md`, the latest growth
   audit (if available).
2. Confirm with the user: pipeline target, activation-rate target,
   expansion-revenue target. Ask for current baselines.
3. Identify the 3-4 most leveraged funnel steps to attack. Propose
   experiments and lifecycle programs against each.
4. Output: stage-by-stage target table, ranked experiment backlog
   (impact × confidence × effort), lifecycle program list.
5. Write to `work/{yyyy-qN-growth-plan}/v{n}.md` and surface to Max
   for cross-domain sign-off before issuing specialist briefs.
</process>
