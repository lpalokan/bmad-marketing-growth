# Brand-Safety & Frequency

<instructions>
Configure brand-safety controls and frequency caps for the campaign.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Per platform:
   - Exclusion lists (negative placements, sensitive content
     categories, competitor brands).
   - Inventory tier (LinkedIn: standard / premium; YouTube: limited
     inventory; Meta: brand-safety filters).
   - Frequency cap (impressions per user per period — cite the
     baseline from the platform's recommendation or the account's
     historical data).
2. For YouTube and Demand Gen: name placement type (in-stream / in-feed
   / shorts) and the rationale.
3. Reporting: brand-safety violation reporting cadence.
4. Write into `v{n}.md`.
</process>
