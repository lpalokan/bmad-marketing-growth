# Attribution Model Recommendation

<instructions>
Recommend the attribution approach for the campaign.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Read brief, `kpis.md`. Identify the campaign objective: demand
   creation, lead capture, or pipeline acceleration.
2. Recommend one of:
   - Lift study (incrementality test): best for demand creation.
   - Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM): best for cross-channel allocation.
   - Multi-touch attribution (MTA): best when objective is per-deal
     credit and journey is fully tracked.
   - Last-click: lowest fidelity; use only when other models impractical.
3. State the trade-offs of the recommendation: cost, time-to-signal,
   data requirements.
4. Name the dashboards / reports needed and who owns them.
5. Write into `v{n}.md`.
</process>
