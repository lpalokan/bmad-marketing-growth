# Joint Co-Marketing Campaign

<instructions>
Produce a joint co-marketing campaign plan.
</instructions>

<process>
1. Read brief, both companies' positioning (theirs from their public
   site).
2. Joint narrative: one paragraph that names a buyer problem both
   companies address; tie outcomes both can claim.
3. Channel mix: per channel, name owner (us / partner / shared),
   asset spec, deadline.
4. Co-branding: both logos, both legal entities, both legal review
   chains.
5. Attribution: sourced vs influenced; both companies agree on rule.
6. Write into `v{n}.md`.
</process>
